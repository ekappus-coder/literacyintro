Phase 1

Pre-alphabetic

The "Visual Cue" stage. Reading is guessing based on environment.

Phase 2

Partial Alphabetic

The "Phonetic Cue" stage. Uses some letter sounds (first/last) to guess.

Phase 3

Full Alphabetic

The "Decoding" stage. Sounds out every letter sequentially.

Phase 4

Consolidated

The "Automatic" stage. Instantly recognizes chunks and whole words.
