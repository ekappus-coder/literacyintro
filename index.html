Professional Focus

As a specialist in Literacy and Language with [Central Wyoming College](https://www.cwc.edu/), I manage the Integrated Education and Training (IET) grant. My work ensures that adult learners not only gain linguistic proficiency but also the technical skills required for the Wyoming workforce.

Grant Leadership

Directing statewide initiatives to integrate basic education with career pathways in Healthcare, Hospitality, and Manufacturing.

Literacy Research

Developing explicit instructional routines for vocabulary growth and applying the Science of Reading to adult education.
