Ehri's Phases of Word Reading

The journey from visual guessing to automatic sight word recognition.

"I read the logo, not the word."
2. Partial Alphabetic
"I see a P... is it Pot?"

"C-a-t... Cat. I sounded it all out."
4. Consolidated Alphabetic
"I see chunks like -ing, -tion, and str- instantly."
