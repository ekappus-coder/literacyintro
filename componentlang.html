Assignment: Key Components of Reading Instruction Presentation & Handout

Role: Teacher Mentor
Target Audience: A new teacher in your chosen grade level (K-6)

Overview

For this assignment, you will become an expert on one essential component of reading instruction. Your goal is to create an engaging, informative resource that teaches a new colleague how to implement this component effectively in their classroom.

Step 2: Choose Your Format

You have full creative freedom to present this information using the media type that best suits your style. Options include:

Step 3: Content Requirements

Your presentation and handout must include the following six elements:

  1. Introduction & Visual: Define the essential component clearly and include at least one relevant visual aid (diagram, infographic, etc.).
  2. Key Research Findings: Summarize the "Science of Reading" behind this component. Why is it critical for student success?
  3. Progression of Skills: Outline the types of skills involved and how they progress across ages or grade levels.
  4. Instructional Recommendations: Provide best practices for teaching this component.
  5. Time Allocation: Specify the recommended amount of instructional time dedicated to this component for your chosen grade.
  6. Instructional Examples: Specific activities, lessons, or strategies a teacher can use tomorrow.
